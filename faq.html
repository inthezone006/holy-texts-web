Frequently asked questions

Answers to common questions about the Holy Texts Android app and upcoming launch plans.

Is Holy Texts available now?

The app is currently in active development. Use the contact page to request early access updates.

Which books are included?

Current design targets include Bible, Quran, Bhagavad Gita, Torah, Guru Granth Sahib, and Dhammapada. Final launch content may vary by release phase.

Can I bookmark verses and continue reading later?

Yes. The app includes bookmark and reading history workflows to support consistent daily reading.

Will there be iOS and web support?

Current priority is Android. Cross-platform support is planned after the initial Android release.

How is user data handled?

We collect only what is necessary to provide account functionality and improve the product experience. See the Privacy page for details.

How can I share feedback?

Use the Contact page to send feature requests, partnership inquiries, or bug reports.
