Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 4, 2026

1. Information We Collect

Holy Texts collects limited account information such as name and email address for secure authentication, profile management, bookmarks, and cloud sync features.

2. Data Security

Data is encrypted in transit using HTTPS/TLS. Holy Texts uses Firebase services (Auth, Firestore, Analytics, and Crashlytics) to support app functionality and improve reliability.

3. Account Deletion

You can request deletion of your account and associated cloud data at any time through our Support Page.

4. Scripture Content

Holy Texts provides access to scripture content for study and reflection, including supported translations and reader settings such as themes, typography, and dual-text Quran display.
